Studio, Theatre, Early Music

Sophie is a woodind multi-instrumentalist based in London, originally from Cork, Ireland. She records for sessions, performs in theatre productions and plays early music.

Woodwind Remote Recording

Do you need woodwind tracks recorded for your project? Sophie is a master in the world of remote recording, and brings a unique flair to everything she does. Get a free consultation with Sophie to see how she could take your music to a new level.

There are boundless possibilities for new sounds with woodwind instruments, especially when layering different lines together to create unique and exciting textures. Sophie records existing lines or can help to compose woodwind parts.

Sophie's Career

Sophie performs primarily as a musician for West End shows, alongside being very active in the UK and European early music scenes. The best thing about West End shows is the variety of instruments and styles that she has to play on a daily basis, all to the highest professional standard. Sometimes, her musical theatre and early music branches colide, as with recent musical Treason. Check out the track below, Take Things Into Our Own Hands, from the cast recording (listen out for the recorder!).

Lux Musicae

Sophie is a founding member of Lux Musicae London, a broken consort dedicated to exploring music from the late Renaissance and Early Baroque periods. With instrumental line up featuring recorders, viola da gamba, harp and lute, as well as two singers (soprano and tenor), their programmes seek to recreate musical performances from the 16th and early 17th centuries, while tracing the patterns of musical influences present during this turbulent period of European history.
